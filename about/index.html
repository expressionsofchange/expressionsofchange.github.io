About

This site documents the idea of “Expressions of Change” using blog articles and demo programs.

To clarify what these “Expressions of Change” are, let’s make some observations and a hypothesis:

  1. All computer programs (except unsuccessful ones) will be changed; managing these changes themselves is a core part of the problem space of Software Development.

  2. Well designed systems will reserve a central role for in the solution space for core aspects of the problem space.

  3. (Follows from 1 & 2): We need to put the changes to our programs more central in the Software Development process and tooling. We’ll start by defining some Expressions of Change: ways of precisely (and possibly automatically) reasoning about changes.

This site documents some experiments with such Expressions of Change in the form of various articles and small demo programs. Everything presented here is work in progress.
